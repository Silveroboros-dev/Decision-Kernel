Decision Kernel MVP

Verification layer for milestone-gated capital release in hybrid human-agent teams.

One narrow loop. A contributor submits a milestone claim, evidence is compressed into structured signal, policy and spend checks run, routine cases auto-clear or escalate, the decision is logged, and later outcomes rescore future trust.

Current demo status: real interactive local prototype, simulated milestone and spend traces, no live sponsor workflow integration yet.
